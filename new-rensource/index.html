What we do

Rensource is in the business of:

Developing Solar Project

Rensource supports project originators develop solar projects by providing EPC financing, deal structuring & PPAs expertise.

Providing Solar EPC Services

Rensource provides world class engineering, procurement & construction services to solar projects. With our EPC expertise, we use world class design & construction tools to build quality PV facilities.

Operating Solar Facilities

Rensource ensures PV plants are efficiently operated & maintained in order to reduce energy spending significantly by providing world class remote monitoring and field maintenance services

Enerdeal

Connecting Developers and Investors

A platform that connects project developers and investors in the renewable energy sector. Are you a developer or an investor? Watch this space for a myriad of opportunities.
